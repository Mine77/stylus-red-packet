Stylus vs Solidity

Solidity

The EVM Standard

Strengths

  • Native EVM integration & simplicity

  • Mature ecosystem & extensive tooling

  • Widely audited patterns & libraries

Limitations

  • Domain-specific language (high learning curve)

  • Limited standard library support

  • Heavy cryptography is gas-prohibitive

  • Smaller global talent pool vs. general languages

Stylus (Rust)

High-Performance WASM

Strengths

  • Full Rust language: ownership & memory safety

  • Access to entire Cargo crate ecosystem

  • Advanced cryptography is feasible & cheap

  • Familiar to millions of systems engineers

Limitations

  • Newer ecosystem with fewer patterns

  • WASM-specific constraints to manage

  • Must understand gas metering model

Solidity optimizes for EVM-native simplicity.
Stylus optimizes for expressive, high-performance Rust logic.
