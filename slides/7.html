WebAuthn: What & Why

Bringing standard internet security to verifiable blockchain execution.

What is WebAuthn?

The W3C Authentication Standard

  • Open Standard

    A widely adopted W3C standard for public-key authentication, supported by all modern browsers.

  • Platform Authenticators

    Uses hardware you already own: TouchID, FaceID, Android Biometrics, or YubiKeys.

  • Phishing Resistant

    Uses public/private key pairs scoped to domains. No shared secrets or passwords to steal.

Why on a Verifiable Runtime?

Unlock Smart Contract Utility

  • Verifiable Logic

    Turn identity assertions into onchain logic. Verify P-256 signatures directly in Rust contracts.

  • Remove Trusted Backends

    Perform auth-gated actions (withdrawals, voting) without a centralized server managing sessions.

  • Better UX than Wallets

    Users sign transactions with a fingerprint scan instead of a browser extension or seed phrase.
